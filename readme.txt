A plugin to display your social accounts fans, subscribers, followers and like buttons on your widget.

== Description ==

With <strong>Themes4WP Social Counter Widget</strong> you can show your social networks statistic and like buttons easily. A perfect plugin to show your social media stats. All you have to do is either use a widget to display your social media counter right on your website in your chosen location!

<strong>Social Networks you can select</strong>
<ul>
	<li>1. Facebook</li>
	<li>2. Twitter</li>
	<li>3. Google Plus</li>
	<li>4. Google Plus like button</li>
	<li>5. Facebook like button</li>
  <li>6. Posts counter</li>
  <li>7. Comments counter</li>
  <li>8. RSS feed</li>
</ul>

<strong>Some features</strong>
<ul>
	<li>Show Social Counter</li>
  <li>Facebook like button</li>
  <li>Google plus like button</li>
	<li>Choose up to 5 buttons styles</li>
  <li>Fully responsive</li>
  <li>User friendly</li>
</ul>

== Installation ==

1. Upload the folder 'themes4wp-social-counter-widget' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Appearance -> Widgets activate and setup 'Themes4WP social counter widget'
